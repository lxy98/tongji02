<<Sympathy for the peasants>>

                        Hoeing millet in mid-day heat,

                        Sweat dripping to the earth beneath.

                        Do you know the food on your plate,

                        Each grain was hard-earned.

                        <<静夜思>>

                        床前明月光，

                        疑是地上霜。

                        举头望明月，

                        低头思故乡。
